Questions, answered.

Is this hiding bad reviews from Google? Isn't that against the rules?
Nope. Customers can still leave any review they want on Google directly — we're not blocking them. All we do is give them a simple choice first: "How was your experience?" If they had a great time, we make it easy to share. If something went wrong, we give them a place to tell you first. They're still free to go to Google. Most don't, because you've already heard them out.
How long does setup take?
About 60 seconds. Pick your business from the dropdown, add your email, hit submit, and your review page is live. We email you the link plus a printable QR card you can put on your counter or hand to customers.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account. No phone calls, no forms, no questions asked. Monthly subscribers stop billing immediately. Annual subscribers can request a prorated refund.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?
If you can copy and paste a link, you can use All5Starz. There's no app to install, no software to learn, and no settings to configure. We do all the setup for you.
Where do bad reviews go?
Straight to your inbox. You'll get an email with the customer's rating, feedback, and (optionally) their email so you can reach out and make it right.
Can I see what my review page will look like before signing up?
Yes! Visit 1to5reviewz.com/r/demo to see a live sample page. Yours will look exactly like that, but with your business name and review link.
